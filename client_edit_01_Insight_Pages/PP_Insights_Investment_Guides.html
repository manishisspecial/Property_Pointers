Guide 1 — ROI Framework
How to Calculate Property ROI India

The PropertyPointers ROI framework used by serious investors and NRIs. Simple, actionable, India-specific.

1
Gross Rental Yield
(Annual Rent ÷ Property Value) × 100. Example: ₹20,000/month rent on ₹60L flat = (2,40,000 ÷ 60,00,000) × 100 = 4% gross yield.
2
Net Rental Yield
Gross yield minus annual expenses (maintenance ₹24K, property tax ₹5K, insurance ₹3K). Net yield on same example = 3.4%.
3
Annual Appreciation
Property value increase per year in %. Noida +14%, Hyderabad +17%, Bangalore +14%, Mumbai +8%. Verify with PropertyPointers City Reports.
4
Combined ROI
Net yield + Appreciation. Hyderabad HITEC: 4.5% yield + 17% appreciation = 21.5% combined ROI. Compare with FD at 6-7%.
5
Break-even Year
Year in which total appreciation gain > total rent paid. In Hyderabad at 17% appreciation: break-even at year 3-4.

Guide 4 — NRI Buying
NRI Property Buying Guide India 2026

Complete legal and financial guide for NRIs buying property in India. FEMA simplified, TDS explained, best cities identified.

⚖️
FEMA Rules for NRI
  • ›NRIs can freely buy residential + commercial property
  • ›Agricultural land requires special RBI permission
  • ›Joint purchase with Indian resident permitted
  • ›No limit on number of properties
🏦
NRI Home Loan
  • ›Available at same rates as residents (8.25–9.25%)
  • ›Max tenure 30 years for NRIs
  • ›NRE/NRO account mandatory
  • ›CIBIL score required — build before applying
💰
TDS on NRI Purchase
  • ›Buyer must deduct 20% TDS on purchase price
  • ›TDS deposited via Form 26QB within 30 days
  • ›Form 16B issued to seller within 15 days
  • ›Lower TDS certificate possible from IT dept
📄
Power of Attorney
  • ›Notarised POA allows trusted person to buy on your behalf
  • ›Must be registered at Indian consulate
  • ›India-attested document required
  • ›Specific POA recommended — not general POA
🔄
Repatriation of Funds
  • ›Sale proceeds repatriable up to USD 1 million/year
  • ›Must be from NRE account funds originally
  • ›CA certificate required for repatriation
  • ›Capital gains tax applicable on profit
🏆
Best Cities for NRI
  • ›Bangalore — IT demand, strong appreciation
  • ›Hyderabad — best yield + ROI combination
  • ›Pune — affordable IT belt with good yields
  • ›Noida — highest volume, multiple price points
