About This Property

Spacious 3 BHK apartment in the prestigious Prestige City township, located on Noida Expressway — one of the fastest-growing residential corridors in NCR. The apartment offers excellent cross-ventilation, a modern kitchen with premium fittings, and a large master bedroom with attached bathroom and walk-in wardrobe. The society features 80% open spaces, a 5-acre central park, and uninterrupted Yamuna floodplain views.

Sector 150 is well-connected to the Delhi-Noida-Direct flyway, Aqua Metro Line, and Jewar International Airport (opening 2025). The locality has seen consistent price appreciation of 12–14% YoY over the past 3 years.
