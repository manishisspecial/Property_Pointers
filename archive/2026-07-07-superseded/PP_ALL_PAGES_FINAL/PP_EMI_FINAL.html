Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

How is home loan EMI calculated?⌄
Our EMI calculator uses the standard reducing balance method used by all Indian banks. Simply enter your loan amount, select your bank and choose the tenure — your exact monthly EMI is calculated instantly. No spreadsheets needed.
What is the SBI home loan rate in 2026?⌄
SBI starts at 8.40% p.a. floating in May 2026. Rates vary by credit score, amount and employment. Verify directly at sbi.co.in before finalising.
How much EMI for ₹50 lakh home loan?⌄
At 8.75% for 20 years: ₹44,017/month. At 8.40% for 20 years: ₹43,168/month. Use the calculator above for your exact figures.
What is a good EMI to income ratio?⌄
Banks allow max 50% FOIR. Financial advisors recommend keeping housing EMI under 30% of net income for financial comfort and flexibility.
Does prepayment reduce EMI or tenure?⌄
Most banks reduce tenure by default. You can request EMI reduction. Reducing tenure saves more — a ₹5L prepayment in year 3 on ₹75L can save ₹12–18L total.
What are hidden costs in a home loan?⌄
Processing fee (0.5–1%), stamp duty on agreement, legal and valuation charges, MODT charges and insurance — typically 1–2% extra. Always ask for a full cost sheet before signing.
