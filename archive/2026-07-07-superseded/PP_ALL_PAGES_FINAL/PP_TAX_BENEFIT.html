Key Tax Rules to Know

Section 24B — Rs 2 Lakh Limit
Interest paid on home loan is deductible up to Rs 2L per year for self-occupied property. No limit for let-out property.
Section 80C — Rs 1.5 Lakh Limit
Principal repayment is deductible under 80C (shared with other 80C investments like ELSS, PF, LIC).
Section 80EE — Extra Rs 50,000
First-time buyers whose loan is under Rs 35L and property value under Rs 50L get an additional Rs 50,000 deduction.
Joint Loan — Double the Benefit
Both co-borrowers can claim independently — Rs 4L total on interest and Rs 3L total on principal in one financial year.
