India's real estate market in 2026 is in a strong upcycle. Residential prices are rising 10–18% annually in key markets. Demand is driven by IT sector expansion, infrastructure development and post-pandemic upgrade buying. Hyderabad, Noida and Bangalore are the top-performing markets with the best combined ROI.

Hyderabad leads with 14–18% annual price appreciation followed by Noida at 12–18% and Bangalore IT corridor at 12–15%. These cities combine strong IT employment, infrastructure growth and relatively affordable entry prices compared to Mumbai and Delhi.
