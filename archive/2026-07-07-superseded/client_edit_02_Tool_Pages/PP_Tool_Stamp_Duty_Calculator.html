Stamp Duty FAQ

Stamp duty in UP (Noida, Greater Noida, Lucknow) is 7% for men and 6% for women. Registration charges 1% extra. For ₹50 lakh property: ₹4 lakh total for men, ₹3.5 lakh for women.

Delhi stamp duty is 6% for men, 4% for women. Plus 1% registration. Women buyers save ₹1 lakh on a ₹50 lakh property. Delhi offers one of the largest gender concessions in India.

Maharashtra Budget 2026 reduced women buyers' stamp duty to 3% (from 5%). Men pay 5-6%. For ₹1 crore property: men pay ₹7L total, women pay ₹4L total — ₹3L saving.

No. Stamp duty and registration must be paid from own funds — cannot be added to the loan. Always budget 7-9% of property value as additional cash requirement beyond the down payment.

Telangana (Hyderabad) has one of the lowest — 5% + 0.5% registration = 5.5% total. Maharashtra women buyers now pay 4% total (3% + 1%) after Budget 2026.

Stamp duty is paid on whichever is higher — agreement price or government circle rate. Our Circle Rate Check feature (free login) shows official circle rate before you register.
