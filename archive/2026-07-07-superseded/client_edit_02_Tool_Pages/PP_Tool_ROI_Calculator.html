Property ROI FAQ

Combined ROI of 10-15% annually is good. Hyderabad and Bangalore IT corridors deliver 15-23% in 2026. Compare with FD at 6.5-7.5% — top property markets significantly outperform over a 5+ year horizon.

Hyderabad HITEC City leads at 18-23% combined ROI (4.5% yield + 17% appreciation). Followed by Noida Expressway at 15-22% and Bangalore Sarjapur Road at 15-19%. These cities combine strong IT employment with affordable prices.

In IT corridors, property outperforms FD 2-3× over 5 years. Key advantages: leverage (control ₹1Cr with ₹20L down), rental income, inflation protection and tax benefits. Liquidity is the main limitation vs FD.

Combined ROI = [(Annual Rent − Annual Costs + Annual Appreciation) / Property Value] × 100. Our calculator computes this automatically for any holding period from 3 to 10 years.

Most Indian markets require 3-5 year holding to overcome transaction costs (7-9% stamp duty + brokerage). Markets with 12-18% appreciation achieve break-even in 2-3 years. Plan for minimum 5 years.
