How It Works

How We CalculateThe Score.

40%
On-Time Delivery
Announced possession dates vs actual handover, across all past projects. Hardest to fake, most buyer-relevant.
25%
Buyer Ratings
Verified purchase reviews only — from buyers who closed a deal. Weighted by recency.
20%
RERA Compliance
Active RERA registration, no penalties, no consumer court orders, no regulatory flags.
15%
Track Record
Years in business, total projects delivered, presence across cities, financial health signals.
Important: Scores are updated quarterly. No builder can pay to improve their score — it is calculated solely from verified data. A builder who consistently delivers on time will always score higher than one who does not, regardless of advertising budget.
