About DLF Limited

DLF Limited is India's largest publicly listed real estate developer, with over 75 years of creating India's urban landscape. Founded in 1946 by Chaudhary Raghvendra Singh, DLF pioneered the development of modern Gurugram and has since developed across 15 states and 24 cities.

The company focuses on residential, commercial and retail properties. DLF is known for premium projects like DLF Privana, The Arbour and The Camellias. Their commercial portfolio includes DLF Cyber City, one of India's largest IT office parks.
