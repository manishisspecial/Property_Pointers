Buyer's Guide

5 Things to Check Before Choosing a Builder

01

RERA Registration

Always verify on official state .gov.in RERA portal before any payment.

02

Delivery Track Record

On-time % tells you more than any brochure. Below 75% is a red flag.

03

Buyer Reviews

Read verified buyer reviews - not marketing testimonials.

04

Financial Health

Large outstanding loans increase delay risk significantly.

05

Project Approvals

Verify building plan, environmental clearance and RERA registration.
